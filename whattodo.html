if a person has covid,he/she should do the following things:-
1)he/she should stay in home or go to a hospital,he/she should not leave his/her home
2)he/she should take the medicines prescribed to them.
3)he/she should not touch anything and eat outside food.
4)he/she should not be in physical contact with his parents/family members.
5)he/she should take rest until he/she are not feeling well.
